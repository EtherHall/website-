About EtherHall

EtherHall started with one person and a dream — a salaried retail job by day, and AION — the Artificially Intelligent Operating Nexus by night. Midnight hours, a closet workstation, and the belief that computers could be more than tools — they could be partners.

From those roots, EtherHall became a company with a simple aim: give people back control over their machines.

Built for the community at large

AION grows step by step with its users. Early backers helped shape it, and the community will always have a voice.

Built for local-first

Your data stays yours. No subscriptions. No lock-in. AION runs on your hardware, not someone else’s cloud.

Built to scale and grow

From one workstation to every household, AION is built for real-world needs today and ready for what’s next.

Built with portability in mind

Run it from a portable SSD if you don’t want to replace your current OS. Boot anywhere and carry your workspace with you.

What we value

  • Ownership — your data and tools belong to you.
  • Simplicity — fewer knobs, more results.
  • Transparency — clear behavior, no mystery calls home.
  • Community — built for and with the people who believe in local AI.
