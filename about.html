About EtherHall

Built after dark, tested in real life, and carried forward by every backer who believes in a brighter future with Local AI.

AION — Artificially Intelligent Operating Nexus

AION is designed to turn your PC into a local AI that grows with you. You interact with your AI; your AI performs actions or guides you through them. Your AI is your operating system.

How EtherHall started

It began with one person, working a salaried retail job and building AION in the midnight hours with a simple intent: make powerful, private AI useful for everyday people.

Built for the community at large

AION grows step by step with its community. Your usage and feedback shape what ships.

Local-first

Runs on your machine. Data stays with you. Network features are opt-in and transparent.

Built to scale and grow

From one workstation to every household that wants it.

Portable & interchangeable

Works off a portable SSD if you don’t want to replace your current OS yet.

What we value

  • Ownership — your data and tools belong to you.
  • Simplicity — fewer knobs, more results.
  • Transparency — clear behavior, no mystery calls home.
