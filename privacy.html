Overview

EtherHall respects your privacy. AION — Artificially Intelligent Operating Nexus — is designed to run locally. Website data collection is intentionally minimal and transparent.

What we collect

  • Contact submissions: If you send us a message, we store your name, email, and message.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: If you subscribe, we store your email with our email provider.
  • Basic logs: Our host may record standard server logs (IP, user-agent, request path).

How we use data

  • To reply to inquiries and provide support.
  • To send opt-in updates and product announcements.
  • To maintain site reliability and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We share only with service providers strictly as needed to operate the site (e.g., email delivery) under contractual privacy terms.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe links are included in all marketing emails.
  • Contact hello@etherhall.com to request access or deletion.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data we hold.
